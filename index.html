Frequently Asked Questions

What makes this protection different?
The system creates digital fingerprints using perceptual hashing—capturing visual essence, not exact pixels. When scammers modify images with color changes or compression, the system sees through the disguise by comparing fingerprints with a similarity threshold. It's not just blocking—it's understanding what makes something harmful.
How does trust grow within a community?
Trust is built together. When moderators flag suspicious content, the system learns from each report. Every identified pattern strengthens the protection for everyone—creating a shared shield that grows smarter with your community's vigilance.
What happens when threats appear?
Matching images are deleted instantly. The system tracks behavior—three blocked posts trigger an automatic ban. A /purge command can scan historical messages to clean up old threats. Protection runs 24/7 without manual intervention.
Can I trust this with my community's privacy?
The system only examines digital fingerprints—16-character hashes that represent visual patterns without storing actual images or personal data. These mathematical representations can't be reversed. No message content or conversations are stored. Your conversations remain yours.
